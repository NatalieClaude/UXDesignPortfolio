CyberSmarties

Since August 2024, I have been working with CyberSmarties Ltd., an innovative educational platform focused on promoting safe social media practices among children. In my role, I led a comprehensive redesign of their platform to enhance user-friendliness, visual appeal, and engagement, ensuring the experience is accessible and appealing to young users. Due to a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), I am unable to share specific project details, but my contributions involved extensive user-centered design improvements and visual refinements tailored to support CyberSmarties' mission of creating a safe, positive online environment for kids. As I continue to grow in my UX career, I am exploring new opportunities where I can leverage my skills and expertise in a dynamic, rewarding environment.
