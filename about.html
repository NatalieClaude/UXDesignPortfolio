About Me

I graduated from the MSc Interactive Media at University College Cork, Ireland, in March 2023, where I discovered my passion for Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

Thesis Project

My thesis project focused on increasing engagement among children at the Cork Public Museum. I designed and developed an interactive, tangible, mixed reality escape room tailored to complement a specific museum exhibition. The game incorporated Tangibles to engage children and link the on-site museum experience with an interactive post-visit app.

Further Education

To further my skills and knowledge in HCI, I completed a Professional Diploma in UX Design from the UX Design Institute, Ireland, in October 2023. My final project focused on improving and redesigning the online hotel booking experience.

Professional Experience

Since August 2024, I have been working with CyberSmarties Ltd., an educational platform dedicated to promoting safe social media use among children. In this role, I have led a redesign of their platform to improve user-friendliness, visual appeal, and engagement for young users. Additionally, I have been contributing to a compliance app project aimed at streamlining the compliance process for independent learning schools in the United States, ensuring an intuitive and accessible user experience that meets industry standards. With these projects now coming to an end and nearly two years of combined experience from professional roles, academic projects, and case studies in UX design, I am excited to transition into an Associate UX Designer position to apply my skills and take on new challenges.
