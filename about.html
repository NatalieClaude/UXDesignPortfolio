About Me

I graduated from the MSc Interactive Media at University College Cork, Ireland, in March 2023, where I discovered my passion for Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

Thesis Project

My thesis project focused on increasing engagement among children at the Cork Public Museum. I designed and developed an interactive, tangible, mixed reality escape room tailored to complement a specific museum exhibition. The game incorporated Tangibles to engage children and link the on-site museum experience with an interactive post-visit app.

Further Education

To further my skills and knowledge in HCI, I completed a Professional Diploma in UX Design from the UX Design Institute, Ireland, in October 2023. My final project focused on improving and redesigning the online hotel booking experience.

Professional Experience

CyberSmarties Ltd.

During my short-term internship at CyberSmarties Ltd., I led a platform redesign to improve usability and engagement for users. My work was highly valued, contributing to the company's mission of providing safe, educational online environments for children.

Independent Study Compliance App

I am currently designing a compliance management app to simplify workflows for educators and administrators for indepependent study in the US. This project involves creating user-centered solutions to meet complex regulatory requirements.

Eager To Grow

I am eager to apply my skills in a full-time UX/Product Designer role, where I can grow further and make meaningful contributions to a team.
