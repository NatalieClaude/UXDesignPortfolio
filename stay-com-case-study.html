Stay.com - Hotel Booking Platform Redesign

An Enhanced Hotel Booking Experience

Process

The UX design process involved five key stages: research, analysis, design, prototyping, and evaluative testing. Each phase contributed to refining and improving the booking experience on Stay.com.

Research

The research phase involved competitive benchmarking, user surveys, and usability testing to identify user pain points and potential areas for improvement.

Analysis

Through affinity mapping and user journey analysis, we identified key issues in the booking process, including navigation challenges, information overload, and confusion around pricing.

Design

The design phase focused on solving the identified problems by creating wireframes and mockups. The layout was simplified, and key actions were made more intuitive.

Prototype

An interactive prototype was created using Figma to simulate the user experience and gather further feedback.

Evaluative Testing

We conducted usability tests to assess the effectiveness of the redesigned platform. Feedback from users helped guide the final adjustments to the interface.
